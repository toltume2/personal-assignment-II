USER GUIDELINES

Instructions for Installing the Application and Setting Up an Account via Email.

Microsoft Outlook - Email Setup Guide

Follow the steps to add your email account to Microsoft Outlook.

Step 1: Open Microsoft Outlook

Click the Outlook icon on your computer. If not installed, download Outlook from Microsoft Office website. Go to Download

Step 2: Add a New Account

Click File → Account Settings → Add Account.

Step 3: Enter Your Email Address

Type your full email address and click Connect.

Step 4: Choose Account Type

Choose IMAP for syncing emails or POP if you only use one device.

Step 5: Enter Password

Type your email password carefully, then click Connect.

Step 6: Manual Server Settings (Optional)

If needed, enter Incoming/Outgoing server addresses manually.

Step 7: Finish Setup

When setup is complete, click Done to start using Outlook.

Mozilla Thunderbird - Email Setup Guide

Follow these simple steps to add your email account to Thunderbird.

Step 1: Open Mozilla Thunderbird

Click the Thunderbird icon. If you don't have it, download it from the Mozilla website. Go to Download

Step 2: Create a New Account

Click Email under the "Set up an account" section.

Step 3: Enter Your Email Details

Fill in your name, email address, and password. Then click Continue.

Step 4: Manual Configuration

Click Manual Config if automatic setup fails. Enter server details (IMAP or POP, SMTP settings).

Step 5: Confirm and Finish

Click Done after the settings are verified.

Step 6: Ready to Use

Your email account is now set up. You can start sending and receiving emails.

Gmail - Email Setup Guide

Follow these steps to set up your Gmail account for easy access.

Step 1: Open Gmail

Click on the Gmail icon or open Gmail website: Go to Download

Step 2: Sign In

Click Sign in at the top right of the page.

Step 3: Enter Your Email Address

Type your full Gmail address (e.g., yourname@gmail.com), then click Next.

Step 4: Enter Your Password

Enter your password and click Next to proceed.

Step 5: Security Alert (Optional)

If a security check or verification is required, follow the on-screen prompts to verify your identity (e.g., 2-Step Verification).

Step 6: Finish Setup

Once you’ve signed in, your Gmail inbox will be ready to use! Start sending and receiving emails.
